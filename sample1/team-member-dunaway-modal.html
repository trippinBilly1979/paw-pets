Owner / Vet

Dr. Teri Morgan

- Bio -

Dr. Morgan has always been an avid animal lover. She had many beloved pets a girl. As a teen, she volunteered at the Scovill Zoo and was later employed as a keeper while attending college.

- Education -

After graduating from Mt. Zion high school in 2000, she studied animal science at the University of Illinois and went on to graduate from the College of Veterinary Medicine in 2006.
