Dr. Dunaway

Dr Dunaway is a cental Illinois native who wanted to be a veterinarian before she can remember. She grew up watching Emergency Vets on Animal Planet and her world has never been the same.

She graduated with her undergraduate degree from Trinity Christian college in 2014 and her veterinary degree from Ross University in 2018 after completing her clinical year at the University of Illinois.

When not at the clinic, Dr. Dunaway enjoys reading, creative writing, and spending time with her labrador Niamh and her cat Alexa.
